Platform Overview
What Is Apex Data?

Apex Data is ScaleLogix AI's proprietary intent measurement platform, engineered to surface high-intent B2B organizations before they raise their hand through conventional channels.

It operates as a second-level blended intent system — aggregating behavioral signals across multiple enterprise-grade data sources and applying advanced analysis to keyword activity, engagement patterns, and purchase indicators.

Methodology

Blended Intent Signals
Combines keyword behavior, engagement patterns, and purchase indicators across multiple data sources — higher accuracy than any single-source intent feed.
Second-Level Methodology
Goes beyond surface signals to identify organizations in active evaluation mode — not passive awareness. The result is a higher-converting, in-market audience.
Continuously Upgraded Infrastructure
ScaleLogix AI audits and improves the underlying data stack on an ongoing basis. You are always running against best-of-breed tooling and data sources.
Treat Lists as Strategic Assets
Blended intent audiences are among the most expensive and most effective in SMB outreach. These are not commodity lists — prioritize and manage them accordingly.
Before You Begin
Two Critical Best Practices

Review these before running your first Apex Data query. Skipping either step can compromise the quality of your intent audience.

1
Apply Business Filters First
Always configure your business filters before applying intent filters. There is a known interaction in one of the underlying data tools that can inadvertently suppress valid intent signals if filters are applied out of sequence. Business filters first — always.
2
Do Not Use NAICS or SIC Codes
NAICS and SIC code filters are not compatible with the Apex Data workflow and should be avoided entirely. Using them within this pipeline will produce unreliable audience outputs. Use alternative segmentation methods as shown in the walkthroughs.
